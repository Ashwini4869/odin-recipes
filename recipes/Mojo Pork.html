Mojo Pork

Description

This mojo pork is super good and super easy...

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 1/2) pound boneless pork butt, trimmed of excess fat and cut into big chunks
  • ¾ cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup lime juice
  • 1 onion, cut into big chunks

Steps

  1. Combine pork, chicken broth, orange juice, lime juice, lemon juice, onion, jalapeno, garlic, salt, oregano, black pepper, cumin, paprika, and bay leaves in a slow cooker. Stir to combine.
  2. Cover and cook on Low until pork shreds easily with a fork, 8 to 10 hours.
